Week 4

SVG and CSS3 Animations

Animate this SVG!

  • Heart Beat: The SVG is attracted to you - you're making its heart race!
  • Bounce: The SVG is excited - it wants to jump around!
  • Fade Out: You've offended the SVG - it needs to step outside for air.
Want to see more? Try pushing its buttons....

  • Left Square: Clicking the left square will cause it to flip out of frame and back.
  • AVL Text: Clicking the "AVL" text will cause it to enlargen and shake temporarily.
  • Right Square: Clicking the right square will cause it to flash.
